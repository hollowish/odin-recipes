Herby Tahini Sauce

Description

Tahini Noodles combine soba noodles slathered in chili oil with vegetables in a garlicky tahini sauce for a quick, healthy dish perfect for lunch or a light dinner on hot summer days.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup tahini
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed or grated
  • 2 T. lemon or lime juice
  • Zest of one lemon or lime
  • 1 T. toasted sesame oil
  • 1 cup parsley &/or cilantro, finely chopped
  • salt & pepper to taste

Steps

  1. combine ingredients in a medium bowl and mix until thoroughly combined.
