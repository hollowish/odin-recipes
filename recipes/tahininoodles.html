Tahini Noodles

Description

Tahini Noodles combine soba noodles slathered in chili oil with vegetables in a garlicky tahini sauce for a quick, healthy dish perfect for lunch or a light dinner on hot summer days.

Ingredients

  • XX Soba noodles
  • 1 T. Chili oil
  • Herby tahini sauce
  • xx broccoli
  • 1 carrot, sliced into 2" long, thin batons
  • Baked tofu

Steps

  1. Cook soba noodles per package instructions. Usually, they need to be boiled for 4-5 minutes, then strained and rinsed in cold water. Once strained, put the soba in serving bowls and coat with Chili oil.
  3. Allow to cool, then store in the refrigerator for up to one week.
