Baked Tofu

Description

This savory baked tofu makes for a great protein that can be added to noodle or rice dishes.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz. firm tofu
  • 1 T. toasted sesame oil
  • 1 T. tamari or soy sauce
  • 1/2 T. rice wine vinegar
  • 1 T. red pepper flakes
  • 1 garlic cloves, crushed or grated
  • 1 t. coarse ground black pepper
  • 1 T. sesame seeds
  • 1 T. grated ginger (about 1-2" piece, peeled)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°.
  2. Cut tofu into 1/2" squares 1/4" thick
  3. Combine all ingredients in a medium mixing bowl and mix gently until tofu is evenly coated.
  4. Pour bowl onto a parchement paper covered sheet pan and spread out evenly.
  5. Bake for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Remove from oven and cool.
